# atliq-mart-promotional-campaign-analysis


## Project Overview

AtliQ Mart, a leading retail chain operating across Southern India, conducted promotional campaigns during Diwali 2023 and Sankranti 2024 to boost sales of its AtliQ-branded products.

The objective of this project was to evaluate campaign effectiveness and identify the promotions, products, categories, cities, and stores that contributed most to business performance.

This analysis was designed to help management answer key questions such as:

- Were the promotional campaigns successful overall?
- Which promotion strategies delivered the best results?
- Which products and categories responded most positively?
- Which stores and cities generated the highest business impact?
- How can future promotional investments be optimized?

The dashboard provides actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making for future promotional campaigns.

Evaluates the effectiveness of different promotion types.

* ₹500 Cashback generated the highest revenue uplift.

### Purpose

Identifies which categories and products benefited most from promotions.

### Key Insights

* Home Appliances recorded the highest sales uplift(264%).
* Combo products generated the highest revenue contribution (₹158M).
* BOGOF significantly increased Home Appliance sales volume.
* High sales growth did not always translate into high revenue growth.
* AtliQ Home Essential Combo was the strongest-performing product with 136% revenue uplift.

---

# Recommendations

1. Continue leveraging Cashback promotions when the primary objective is revenue growth.

2. Use BOGOF promotions strategically for inventory movement and sales volume growth.

3. Optimize discount-based promotions by aligning discount levels with product pricing and campaign objectives.

4. Prioritize Combo products, which generated the highest promotional revenue.

5. Explore alternative promotional strategies for Home Appliances to improve revenue while maintaining strong customer response.

6. Continue investing in high-performing markets while investigating the causes behind lower-performing regions.
